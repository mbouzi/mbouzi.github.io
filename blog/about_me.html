About Me

My name is Malcolm Bouzi and I currently live in Queens, New York. Being in the music industry had allowed me pursue my desire to connect with people and affect lives through a combination of sound and technology. Afer being introduced to programming by my brother, I discovered that through it I could satisfy that same appetite for meaningful connection in a very impactful way. I'm now attending Dev Bootcamp to embrace this newfound passion for computer programming and challenge myself out of my comfort zone. I am excited about taking my life in this new direction and gaining skills that will allow me to pursue an immensely rewarding career.
