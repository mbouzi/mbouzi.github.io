Values

02/08/2015

When I look back at my life, the times that I have been the happiest, most proud, most satisfied and most inspired have been because of music. I have always been very aware of the power that music holds and that artist hold. The ability to affect emotion, affect thought, and influence belief are all present in sound. Some of my favorite artists have helped instill certain core values in me, and I try support artists that stay true to those values.

I think that my values can certainly help me mediate stereotype threat. One of the main lessons I have learned through music is the importance of staying true to ones self. As long of you have knowledge of self and belief in your ability, any obstacle or situation can be overcome. I hope that having this mentality will help me through the tough times that are to come in Phase 1 and beyond at Dev.
