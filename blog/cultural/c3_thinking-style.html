Thinking Style

01/09/2015

Being aware of your thinking style can make an immense difference in how efficiently you process information and come to understanding it. You can maximize your learning potential, reduce the stress of the learning experience, and overcome the limitions of poor instruction. Knowledge of your thinking style gives you insight into your habit, strengths and weaknesses.

As an Abstract Sequential thinker, I am adept in visulization and using my imagination, and I like to organize information in a linear fashion. I like to analyze situations before making a decision and apply login in finding solutions to problems.
