About

Malcolm Bouzi is a Web Developer from Queens, New York with a passion for music and technology. After graduating fron George Washington University and pursuing a career in audio engineering, he discovered web development.

Since graduating from the intensive web development training program Dev Bootcamp, he's built and deployed apps using Ruby/Rails and Javascript, and continued to get inspired and learn about new developments in the industry such as ReactJS and ES6. Malcolm has worked with some of todays leadning artists and brands, including Britney Spears, Linkin Park, Leon Bridges, Cirque du Soleil and Wicked.

His strong work ethic and commitment to precision, mixed with his passion for creativity, allow him enjoy both ends of the stack. He relishes the challenge of bringing a vision to fruition through the most modular code and elegant interactive experiences he can fashion.
