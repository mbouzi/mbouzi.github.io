About

Malcolm Bouzi is a Web Developer from Queens, New York with a passion for music and technology. He's built and deployed apps using Ruby/Rails and Javascript, and created websites for some of todays leading artists and brands, including Britney Spears, Linkin Park, Leon Bridges, Star Trek and Wicked.

His strong work ethic and commitment to precision, mixed with his passion for creativity, allow him enjoy both ends of the stack. He relishes the challenge of bringing a vision to fruition through the most modular code and elegant interactive experiences he can fashion.
